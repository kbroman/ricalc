
These are the three-point probabilities for 4-way RILs by sibling
mating, as calculated by the perl scripts in the Perl directory.
These are comma-delimited files, suitable for opening within Excel.
They may be imported into R via read.csv().


results4A3NI.csv    Autosomes with no crossover interference.

results4A3I.csv     Autosomes with strong positive crossover
                    interference (the gamma model with nu=11.3).

results4X3NI.csv    The X chromosome with no crossover interference.

results4X3I.csv     The X chromosome with strong positive crossover
                    interference (the gamma model with nu=11.3).
